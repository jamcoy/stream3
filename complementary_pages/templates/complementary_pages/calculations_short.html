Economy calculations

EasyFuelTracker calculates your vehicle's fuel economy using the brim-to-brim method (one full tank to the next full tank).

Partial tanks are included in the calculations if they meet certain conditions.

If you miss logging any refuels, your latest refuel and all preceding refuels to the previous full tank are excluded.

For further details on how we calculate the fuel economy figures, please click here.

To get good results:

  • Always try to fill your tank.

  • Record all refueling events.
